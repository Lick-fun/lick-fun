Items fixed in this session (all verified against live code; full prod
build succeeded):

1. **Profile metadata persistence** — was writing to local disk which
   Railway wipes on every redeploy. Migrated 3 consumers to a new
   Storj-backed `lib/server/profileMetadataStore.ts` mirroring the
   token metadata pattern. Bundled `src/data/profile-metadata.json`
   kept as fallback/seed (2 existing profiles covered).

2. **Vault untracked-balance Telegram alerting** in
   `script/graduation-keeper.ts`. New `checkUntrackedVaultBalance()`
   compares vault balance vs tracked per-token mapping, fires a
   Telegram alert when untracked MON crosses 30 (configurable).
   Deliberately READ-ONLY — sweep() is onlyOwner (multisig) by
   design, an audit hardening that should not be weakened. No-op
   unless ALERT_TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN + ALERT_TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID are set.

3. **Sentry error monitoring** (@sentry/nextjs v10). Wired in all
   three runtimes (client/server/edge) with env-gated init —
   completely no-op if NEXT_PUBLIC_SENTRY_DSN is unset. Root error
   boundary at app/global-error.tsx. withSentryConfig() in
   next.config.ts handles source-map upload (also env-gated).
   Wallet-rejection errors filtered out to avoid noise.

4. **Plausible analytics** — single <Analytics /> component in
   root layout, cookie-less, no-op unless
   NEXT_PUBLIC_PLAUSIBLE_DOMAIN is set.

5. **Terms of Service + Privacy Policy pages** at /terms and
   /privacy, both statically pre-rendered. New site-wide <Footer />
   added to root layout with links to both. Both pages clearly
   marked "DRAFT — pending legal review" until counsel signs off.

6. **README.md** fully refreshed for Phase 4: V2 vault addresses,
   V2 architecture (VaultBuybackBurnV2 + VaultRecouper), Phase 4
   build-pipeline rows, updated test counts (176/15 contracts/11
   pages), new Observability subsection under Security.
